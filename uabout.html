About Me

I am Mark Elrod, but by now I think you've already figured that out. I was born at a very young age, have been growing up ever since, and have filled that time with as many experiences as I can find. I have had the pleasure of meeting a wide variety of people, and look forward to meeting you as well!

As a massive nerd, I enjoy catching Marvel's latest movies, playing a new video game, and figuring out how things work. My own origin story includes highlights such as writing technical manuals for non-technical audiences, and earning my mechanical engineering degree. I love using these technical abilities to turn my ideas into reality, such as designing a website for the Iron, Silver, and Salt podcast, creating my own video games, and publishing my own card game.

So if you're interested in learning more about me, about the animals in my life, or are just interested in chatting about what I can design for you, please do reach out!
