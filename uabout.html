About Me

I am Mark Elrod, but if you're here, you probably already figured that out. I was born at a very young age, and have spent a great deal of time since then creating a wide variety of experiences for others to enjoy. From escape rooms and games to websites and apps, I have worked on a wide variety of projects, and am not shy about bringing my unique experiences to bear.

For me, there's very little as satisfying as watching someone enjoy the thing that you have created. Listening to a group of friends laugh together over a game I've created, or hearing the satisfied sigh of someone whose problem has been assuaged by my designs, is what keeps driving me to create more experiences for the people around me.

Outside of design, I'm frequently experimenting with a new recipe, or trying a new restaurant. I also love music; if I'm in the car, you better believe there are tunes coming along for the ride.

So if you're interested in learning more about me and how I approach design, or are interested in discussing how I can bring my knowledge to bear for you, please do reach out!
