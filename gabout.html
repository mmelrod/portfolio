About Me

I am Mark Elrod, but you probably already figured that out. I am a game designer and a UX designer, having created games such as Tug and Knights of the Cupboard, the latter of which outsold 98% of the other games on The Game Crafters. I've also had the pleasure of joining the ranks of PAX's Enforcer community, helping attendees and exhibitors alike enjoy these annual conventions to their fullest. In everything I do, I make sure I am providing the best experience possible for those around me.

Outside of games, I've worked as a technical writer, creating instruction manuals to teach scientists how to operate the machinery used in molecular analysis. I've also worked as a shift manager at an escape room, using colelcted feedback on the rooms to improve their puzzles and overal design.

Personally, I'm a huge foodie, and love trying new recipes from cultures all around the world. I also love music, having played drums of all kinds ever since elementary school.
