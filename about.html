About Me

Some of my earliest memories are of sitting at the kitchen table with a friend, drawing spaceships, and enthusiastically discussing whose ship would win. Every sheet of brightly colored construction paper became our battlefield, every line another laser, and every box either an engine or a cannon (depending on what we needed it to be at that exact moment in the conversation). In our minds, the battles were the most epic confrontations you could never hope to see.

In the years that have passed, my boundless childhood imagination has been tempered by the constraints of modern technology. It turns out, you would need an incredibly potent power source (and more than a few heat sinks) to power several dozen lasers, 20 cannon/engines, and some form of life support to give the poor pilot the chance to use any of them. But these realities also infected me with something far more potent- the realization that if I played by the rules set by decades of scientific discovery, I could turn these ideas into something real.


This drive to take ideas, both my own and others’, and help shape them into something people get to use and enjoy, has kept me on a path destined for design. For me, there is no greater satisfaction than watching something you helped create be used and enjoyed by the people around you. If at the end of our next project, you also look forward to thinking, “We made that. We molded that idea into reality.”, please feel free to contact me. I would love to help.
