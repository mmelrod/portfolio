Agilent 5977B Operation Manual

This is the operation manual I created in FrameMaker for Agilent's 5977B mass spectrometer. I started by reviewing the operating manual for Agilent's 5975 mass spectrometer with Agilent engineers, to discuss the information's relevancy, accuracy, and organization. To keep all of the updates for the 250+ page manual coordinated, I utilized Adobe Acrobat's annotation and note tools to mark the necessary changes and the status thereof. Once each round of changes was made, I met with Agilent engineers to review the updated manual and ensure the accuracy of the information therein.

Skills

  • FrameMaker
  • Video storyboarding
  • Adobe Illustrator

Challenges

  • Multi-project organization
  • Team coordination
  • Learning operation of the 5977B

Agilent Intuvo 9000 troubleshooting manual

This is the troubleshooting manual for Agilent's Intuvo 9000 gas chromatograph. For this manual, I was brought to work with Agilent on-site, constantly interfacing with Agilent SMEs, engineers, and prototypes of the machine itself.

The unique challenge of this manual was incorporating its information into the touchscreen interface built into the GC. For this, the information had to be as concise as possible, while still displaying important answers to users' questions. Furthermore, each help page had to be thoroughly tested to ensure users were able shown help relevant to the screen they were requesting help from.

Skills

  • FrameMaker
  • HTML
  • QA testing

Challenges

  • Adapting to a new medium
  • Constant client communication

Agilent OpenLab

Here is a series of training videos I created to accompany Agilent's OpenLAB software. The first step to creating them was to communicate with Agilent SMEs to discuss what proceedures were Agilent's highest priority due to their complexity, frequency, and/or marketability. Once I had a list of videos, I researched the procedures necessary in preparation for performing and recording the actions myself. Once I had all of the video and audio necessary, I combined everything in Adobe Premiere before sending Agilent a first draft of the videos for review.

One particularly challenging aspect of this project was learning how to perform the necessary actions well enough to be able to do so in multiple languages, for Agilent's international audiences. However, with the assistance of the Google translate app's live translation feature, I was able to ensure these localized videos were up to Agilent's quality stanards.

Skills

  • Adobe Premiere
  • Software testing
  • Script writing

Challenges

  • Learning OpenLab
  • Multi-lingual video production
