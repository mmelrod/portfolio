Agilent 5977B Operation Manual

This is the operation manual I created in FrameMaker for Agilent's 5977B mass spectrometer. I started by reviewing the operating manual for Agilent's 5975 mass spectrometer with Agilent engineers, to take notes on the information's relevancy, accuracy, and organization. Once each round of changes was made, I met with Agilent engineers to review the updated manual and ensure the accuracy of the information therein.

Alongside this manual, I was also responsible for updating the 5977B's troubleshooting and maintenance, site prep, and installation guides. Since I was working on 4 manuals simultaneously, keeping everything organized was of utmost importance.

  • Adobe Premiere
  • Adobe Illustrator

OpenShot Video Editor Windows User Guide

This is a Windows User Guide for the open source video editing software OpenShot Video Editor. I created this manual from scratch as the capstone project for Ugur Akinci's technical writing course.

The main hurdle I overcame for this manual was the extremely condensed timeline. I had 48 hours to plan and outline content, collect screenshots for graphics, and write all of the language. I overcame this through careful time allocation, analysis of the information for content prioritization, and task organization to keep track of my progress within the timeline.

Agilent Intuvo 9000 troubleshooting manual

This is the troubleshooting manual for Agilent's Intuvo 9000 gas chromatograph. For this manual, I was brought to work with Agilent on-site, constantly interfacing with Agilent SMEs and engineers as well as prototypes of the machine itself.

The unique challenge of this manual was incorporating its information into the touchscreen interface built into the gas chromatograph. This meant that the information had to be as concise as possible, while still displaying important answers to users' questions. Furthermore, each help page had to be thoroughly tested to ensure users were shown help relevant the processes they were attempting.

Tools Used

  • Adobe FrameMaker
  • HTML/CSS
  • Adobe RoboHelp

Agilent OpenLab

Here is a series of training videos I created to accompany Agilent's OpenLab software. The first step to creating them was to communicate with Agilent SMEs to discuss what procedures were Agilent's highest priority due to their complexity, frequency, and/or marketability. Once I had a list of videos, I researched the procedures necessary in preparation for performing and recording the actions myself. Then, Once I had all of the necessary audio and video recordings, I combined everything in Adobe Premiere before sending Agilent a first draft of the videos for review.

One particularly challenging aspect of this project was learning how to perform the necessary actions well enough to be able to do so in multiple languages, for Agilent's international audiences. However, with the assistance of the Google Translate app's live translation feature, I was able to ensure these localized videos were up to Agilent's quality standards.

  • Articulate Storyline
